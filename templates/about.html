Course Project Details

This tennis analytics platform demonstrates the application of modern web development techniques, including:

  • Flask web framework
  • PostgreSQL database
  • User authentication
  • Data visualization
  • Responsive design
  • AJAX interactions
  • RESTful API design
  • Form validation
  • Agile methodology

Our Mission

To democratize professional tennis analysis and make it accessible to players of all levels.

Precision Analysis

Provide detailed, accurate assessment of tennis technique using advanced AI algorithms.

Continuous Improvement

Enable players to track progress and make measurable improvements over time.

Community Learning

Facilitate knowledge sharing between players, coaches, and tennis enthusiasts.

Future Work

Our roadmap for enhancing the TennisAI platform

Real-time notifications

Instant alerts about performance changes, match insights, and improvement recommendations to keep you updated on your progress.

Advanced data analysis and ML integration

Deeper insights through advanced machine learning algorithms that analyze patterns in your play style and provide more personalized recommendations.

Mobile app integration

Access your tennis analytics on the go with our dedicated mobile application, including on-court recording and immediate feedback.

Social features

Connect with other players, share your progress, and compare techniques with a tennis community that helps you improve together.

Coach-student relationships

Enhanced tools for coaches to monitor student progress, create training plans, and provide remote guidance through the platform.

Video analysis

Advanced video processing with frame-by-frame analysis of your technique, comparing your movements with professional players.
