The Technology Behind TennisAI

AI Computer Vision

Our proprietary computer vision algorithms analyze player movements from uploaded videos or photos, detecting 33 key body points to assess technique with incredible precision.

Professional Benchmarking

Your technique is compared against a database of professional players, allowing for detailed comparison against proven successful patterns.

Adaptive Learning

The more you use TennisAI, the better it understands your game. Our system adapts recommendations based on your progress and improvement areas.

Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive statistical analysis breaks down every aspect of your game, from serve placement percentages to forehand speed variations.

The Analysis Process

1
Data Collection

Upload videos or photos of your tennis match. Our system supports various formats and can analyze both match play and practice sessions.

2
AI Processing

Our AI engine processes your uploads, identifying key technical elements like grip position, swing path, foot positioning, and body rotation.

3
Stroke Analysis

Each stroke is broken down into phases: preparation, backswing, contact, and follow-through. Each phase receives detailed scoring and feedback.

4
Personalized Recommendations

Based on your analysis, TennisAI generates tailored improvement tips and specific drills to address technical weaknesses.
